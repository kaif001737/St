A Legacy of Empowerment

St. Antony's Girls Higher Secondary School was established in Mylapore, Chennai, in the year 1950. Founded by the Sisters of the Congregation with a vision to provide quality education to girls, the school has evolved from a small diocesan learning center into a premier higher secondary institution in Tamil Nadu.

Over the last seven decades, we have remained deeply committed to breaking educational barriers for young women. We provide a space where girls are encouraged to raise their voices, ask critical questions, and pursue academic excellence across disciplines.

Our school logo crest represents the triple anchor of our educational philosophy: the open book representing academic illumination, the lamp of knowledge representing inner spiritual guidance, and the lily representing purity, humility, and moral strength. Driven by our motto, "Walk in Light and Truth", we prepare our girls to leave the campus as beacons of hope and integrity for the society.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of holistic learning that nurtures self-reliant, ethical, and intellectually vibrant young women who will contribute meaningfully to the betterment of families, communities, and the global society.

Our Mission

To provide a supportive and challenging learning environment that blends academic rigor with moral integrity. We strive to cultivate leadership skills, spiritual values, scientific thinking, and a sense of social responsibility in every student.

Moral Integrity

Instilling truthfulness, honesty, and transparency in actions and choices.

Pursuit of Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in academics, arts, sports, and life skills.

Service & Compassion

Nurturing respect for all, empathy for the weak, and a readiness to serve.
